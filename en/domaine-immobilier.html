Real Estate Law
With several years of experience and recognized expertise in the field of real estate, we support our clients with rigor and determination, whether they are individuals, professionals, or institutions.
Our team, composed of specialized lawyers and legal experts, offers a complete service covering all facets of real estate law, including:
  • Real Estate Transactions : We advise and assist our clients in all phases of their real estate transactions, whether it involves purchasing, selling, leasing, or financing real estate. Our goal is to secure your transactions and maximize your interests, ensuring compliance with current legislation at every step.
  • Construction Law : We support developers, builders, and buyers in construction projects, from design to completion. We are involved in drafting and negotiating construction contracts, monitoring legal warranties, and managing any potential disputes.
  • Commercial and Residential Leases : Our expertise extends to drafting and revising leases, as well as resolving disputes between landlords and tenants, always prioritizing the search for amicable and effective solutions.
  • Condominium and Syndic Law : We offer legal advice on condominium management, the rights and obligations of co-owners, as well as on relations with condominium syndics.
  • Urban Planning and Land Use : We assist our clients in their efforts to obtain building permits, declare works, and in cases involving disputes related to urban planning law.
Our approach is personalized: we take the time to deeply understand your needs and objectives in order to offer you tailored solutions. We are committed to providing you with the highest quality legal support, offering our technical expertise, our in-depth knowledge of the real estate sector, and our ability to innovate.
For any questions or to make an appointment, do not hesitate to contact us. Our team is at your disposal to help you successfully navigate the complex world of real estate law.
